Our CommitmentTransforming Communities

At Goldman Sachs, we believe in creating a positive impact in the communities we serve through strategic philanthropy, volunteerism, and sustainable initiatives.

Education & Youth Development

We support educational programs and mentorship initiatives to empower the next generation, fostering opportunity and growth in underserved communities.

Sustainability & Environment

Our commitment to sustainability drives investments in renewable energy and environmental initiatives to build a more resilient future.

Entrepreneurship & Economic Empowerment

Through programs like 10,000 Small Businesses, we provide entrepreneurs with the tools and resources to grow their businesses and strengthen local economies.

Employee Volunteerism

Our employees dedicate thousands of hours to community service, partnering with local organizations to drive meaningful change.

Our StoriesImpact in Action

Empowering Youth Through Education

Our partnership with local schools has provided scholarships and mentorship to over 5,000 students in 2025.

Investing in a Sustainable Future

We’ve committed $1 billion to renewable energy projects, reducing carbon emissions across communities.

Supporting Small Businesses

Our 10,000 Small Businesses program has helped create over 20,000 jobs since its inception.
